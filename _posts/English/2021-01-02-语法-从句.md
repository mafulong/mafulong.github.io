### 时态补充: 

1. **主将从现**

i will hit u when you come here

if it rains, i will hit u.

2. **主过从过**

复合句有先后关系，过去+过去完成时, eg. when he came there, the train had gone.

3. **主现从任**

he tells me that u have died
