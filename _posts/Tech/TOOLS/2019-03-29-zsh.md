## 设置默认 zsh

[参考](https://askubuntu.com/questions/131823/how-to-make-zsh-the-default-shell)

You may also do this:

open your bashrc file in your favourite editor

    sudo vim ~/.bashrc

then add the line below top of the file

    exec zsh
